Visit Port Harcourt

Top three activities to do at Port Harcourt

Visit one of our numerous parks

One of the best places to get closer to nature is visit the park which was created to help those that come there to relax and feel the freedom that only nature has to offer

Get to swim at our beach

Swim at the equisite spring beach which is very beautiful and end up making you feel relaxed.

Visit the zoo

Get to see the wonderful animals that live here through the eyes of our zoo that has a collection of the most awesome african animals
